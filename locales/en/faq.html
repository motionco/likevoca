Quickly resolve your questions about using LikeVoca

There are two ways to sign up for LikeVoca:

  • Email Registration: Create an account directly by entering your email address and password.
  • Social Login: Sign up quickly using your Google, Facebook, or Apple account.

After registration, complete email verification to access all features.

Feature OAuth Registration Email Registration
Convenience Instant login with existing account Requires separate password setup
Security Platform's security policies applied Direct security management
Privacy Minimal information sharing Complete privacy control

Password reset process:

  1. Click the "Forgot your password?" link on the login page.
  2. Enter your registered email address.
  3. Click the reset link sent to your email.
  4. Set up a new password.

The reset link is valid for 24 hours only. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder.

Account deletion is irreversible, so please consider carefully:

  1. Go to the Settings page.
  2. Find the "Account Management" section.
  3. Click the "Delete Account" button.
  4. Select a reason for deletion and enter your password.
  5. Your account will be permanently deleted after final confirmation.

All learning data and progress will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered after account deletion.
