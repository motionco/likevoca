Privacy Policy

LikeVoca Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 29, 2025

1. Purpose of Personal Data Processing

LikeVoca (hereinafter "Company") processes personal data for the following purposes. Personal data being processed is not used for purposes other than the following, and when the purpose of use changes, necessary measures such as obtaining separate consent will be implemented in accordance with Article 18 of the Personal Information Protection Act.

  • Membership registration and management
  • Service provision and operation
  • Learning progress management and personalized service provision
  • Customer consultation and complaint handling
  • Service improvement and new service development
  • Marketing and advertising activities (only with consent)

2. Personal Data Processing and Retention Period

The Company processes and retains personal data within the personal data retention and use period consented to when collecting personal data from data subjects or the personal data retention and use period prescribed by law.

  • Member information: Until membership withdrawal
  • Learning records: 1 year after membership withdrawal (for service improvement purposes)
  • Service usage records: 3 months (for statistics and service improvement purposes)

3. Provision of Personal Data to Third Parties

The Company only provides personal data to third parties in cases corresponding to Articles 17 and 18 of the Personal Information Protection Act, such as consent from data subjects or special provisions of law.

The Company may provide personal data to third parties in the following cases:

  • When the data subject has given prior consent
  • When required by law or when requested by investigative agencies according to procedures and methods prescribed by law for investigative purposes
  • When providing personal data in a form that cannot identify specific individuals for purposes such as statistical compilation and academic research

4. Personal Data Processing Outsourcing

The Company outsources the following personal data processing tasks for smooth personal data processing:

  • Cloud service providers: System operation and data storage
  • Email service providers: Service-related email delivery

When concluding outsourcing contracts, the Company specifies matters related to prohibition of personal data processing beyond the purpose of outsourced tasks, technical and administrative protective measures, restrictions on re-outsourcing, management and supervision of contractors, and liability for damages in documents such as contracts in accordance with Article 26 of the Personal Information Protection Act, and supervises whether contractors process personal data safely.

5. Rights and Obligations of Data Subjects and How to Exercise Them

Users can exercise the following rights as personal data subjects:

  • Request for notification of personal data processing status
  • Request for access to personal data
  • Request for correction and deletion of personal data
  • Request for suspension of personal data processing

The exercise of the above rights can be done in writing, email, fax, etc. in accordance with Article 41, Paragraph 1 of the Enforcement Decree of the Personal Information Protection Act, and the Company will take action without delay.

6. Personal Data Items Processed

The Company processes the following personal data items:

Required Items

  • Email/Password Registration: Email address, password (encrypted storage)
  • OAuth Registration: Email address, external authentication provider identifier
  • Common: Service usage records

Optional Items

  • Name or nickname
  • Target learning language
  • Learning progress and achievement data
  • Marketing consent status

Non-Personal Information Collection Items

The following information is classified as non-personal information that cannot identify individuals and is stored locally in the browser:

  • Language settings (localStorage: userLanguage)
  • User interface settings
  • Browser environment settings

Such non-personal information can be directly managed and deleted by users through browser settings.

7. Destruction of Personal Data

The Company destroys personal data without delay when personal data becomes unnecessary due to expiration of the retention period, achievement of processing purposes, etc.

The procedures and methods for destruction are as follows:

  • Destruction procedure: The Company selects personal data for which reasons for destruction have occurred and destroys personal data with approval from the personal data protection officer.
  • Destruction method: Information in electronic file format uses technical methods that cannot reproduce records. Personal data printed on paper is destroyed by shredding or incineration.

8. Measures to Ensure Security of Personal Data

The Company takes the following technical, administrative, and physical measures necessary to ensure security in accordance with Article 29 of the Personal Information Protection Act:

  • Encryption of personal data (encrypted password storage)
  • HTTPS communication encryption
  • Access restrictions to personal data (authentication system)
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

9. Personal Data Protection Officer

The Company designates a personal data protection officer as follows to take overall responsibility for personal data processing and handle complaints and damage relief related to personal data processing:

Personal Data Protection Officer

  • Department: Development Team
  • Email: privacy@likevoca.com
  • Contact: Through inquiry page

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applied from the effective date, and when there are additions, deletions, and corrections of changes according to laws and policies, notice will be given through announcements from 7 days before the implementation of changes.

11. Service Hosting and Data Storage Location

Currently, the LikeVoca service is hosted within the Republic of Korea, and personal data is stored domestically. If it becomes necessary to use overseas servers due to future service expansion, prior notice and consent will be obtained.

This privacy policy is effective from August 29, 2025.
